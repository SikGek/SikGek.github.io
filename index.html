About Me

I was born in South Korea and migrated to the United States when I was 10, and have wished to pursue EE my entire life.

During university, I concentrated on mathematics and fundamentals of machine learning and controls, subsidized by courses in embedded systems, analog and digital circuits, object oriented programming, signal processing and robotics.

Thanks to the EE professors I've built a rapport with in Korea, I was able to gain multiple internships and experiences with admirable startup companies in Korea.

I pursued a M.S. in AI and robotics at UCSD and completed over 50% of the requirements, but put it on hold due to lack of research opportunities and financial constraints.
